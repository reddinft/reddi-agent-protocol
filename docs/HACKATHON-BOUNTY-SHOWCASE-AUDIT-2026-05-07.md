# Hackathon Bounty Showcase Audit — 2026-05-07

## Verdict

Most bounty/ecosystem surfaces are incorporated into the website/demo enough to showcase, but they are not all equally strong. Quasar, x402-compatible payment boundaries, Pay.sh / `reddi-x402` sandbox charge evidence, OpenRouter, Surfpool, and Jupiter-boundary evidence are strong. Umbra is now a strong private-payments adapter-contract lane with direct bounty fit: SDK packages are installed/import-verified, the mocked receiver-claimable UTXO lifecycle generates a selective-disclosure receipt, Umbra devnet encrypted-balance deposit evidence proves a bounded wSOL deposit queue/callback path, and receiver-claimable UTXO devnet-only evidence proves create→scan→claim with receiver encrypted-balance update; mainnet/live-production settlement is not claimed. Torque exists and is now clearer on the homepage. MagicBlock has moved beyond generic boundary proof into **live Quasar-native delegation and bounded agent-vault settlement proof**, but it is still **not a successful arbitrary-wallet/private payee settlement proof**: createPermission/delegatePermission/escrow+vault delegation succeed against MagicBlock programs, patched Quasar PER executes inside MagicBlock TEE, and the 2026-05-08 smoke restores a Quasar-owned AgentVault to base devnet with withdraw-after-settlement evidence; non-delegated payee lamport settlement is not claimed. ElizaOS/SendAI exist as framework adapter evidence, not core on-chain proof.
